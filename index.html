Academic Units

Doctoral Programme

The Doctoral Programme prepares students for careers in teaching and research. This programme leading to the PhD degree is broad-based and involves a minimum course credit requirement and a research thesis.

LEARN MORE

MBA

Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management, IIT Bombay, through its flagship Master of Business Administration programme, seeks to develop managers with an integrated and wholesome understanding of all business functions.

LEARN MORE

EMBA Programme

A partnership between SJMSOM, IIT Bombay, and Washington University in St. Louis. This Executive MBA is the only one in the world to confer a degree from both an Indian and an American university.

LEARN MORE

MDP

Management Development Programmes (MDPs) are aimed at enhancing competencies among managers and executives working at any level. They are offered in different management domains.
